# Consent and Confidentiality Checklist

## Organizational permission

- [ ] Miguel confirms the purpose and boundaries of the research.
- [ ] The organization approves observation locations and timing.
- [ ] Commercially sensitive topics and excluded material are identified.
- [ ] The organization understands that findings may include uncertainty and failed assumptions.

## Participant consent

- [ ] Participation is voluntary.
- [ ] The intended use of notes, quotes, images, and stories is explained.
- [ ] Participants can decline recording or publication without penalty.
- [ ] Separate permission is obtained for names, exact quotes, photos, and identifiable stories.
- [ ] Withdrawal and correction procedures are explained.

## Data handling

- [ ] Participant labels are used by default.
- [ ] Raw recordings are kept outside public exports.
- [ ] Access is limited to the working team.
- [ ] Identifying details are removed from draft manuscript material unless permission is granted.
- [ ] Retention and deletion timing is agreed.

## Publication review

- [ ] Exact quotations are verified against recordings or notes.
- [ ] Permissions are traceable.
- [ ] Private commercial information is removed.
- [ ] Observations are not represented as general proof.
- [ ] The two-week limit is stated clearly.
