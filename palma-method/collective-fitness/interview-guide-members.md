# Interview Guide — Members

Duration: approximately 25–40 minutes  
Format: semi-structured  
Status: draft

## Questions

1. How did you first hear about The Collective, and what made you try it?
2. What made you return after the first visit?
3. Describe a moment here that felt especially welcoming, useful, or memorable.
4. Who do you interact with before, during, and after training?
5. Have you met anyone here whom you now contact or help outside a class?
6. Is it easy to enter conversations and activities here? When is it difficult?
7. Are there unwritten rules or rituals that a new person must learn?
8. Have you contributed something beyond attending? What made that possible?
9. What kinds of introductions, recognition, or activities would feel useful rather than forced?
10. What should The Collective never become?
11. If you stopped coming, what would you miss most?
12. What have I not asked that matters?

## Interviewer reminders

- Ask for concrete episodes, not only opinions.
- Do not imply that deeper participation is inherently better.
- Avoid collecting health or other sensitive personal information unless essential and explicitly consented.
- Confirm permission separately for any exact quote or identifiable story.
