# Working Direction to Test

Treat Relationship Capital as the asset or relational system itself.

Treat the following as properties or dimensions of that asset rather than parts of its shortest core definition:

- trust;
- reciprocity;
- access;
- network position;
- diversity;
- relationship strength;
- institutional reach.

Treat **mobilizability** as a derived property: the practical capacity to activate relationships when information, cooperation, introductions, support, legitimacy, or coordinated action are needed.

Treat business or social outcomes as results produced through the asset, not as parts of the asset itself.
