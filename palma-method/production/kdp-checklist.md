# Amazon KDP Production Checklist

## Rights and metadata

- [ ] Final title, subtitle, author name, and edition match every file.
- [ ] Publishing rights and territories are confirmed.
- [ ] ISBN decision is recorded.
- [ ] Description, keywords, categories, and pricing are approved.
- [ ] Quotes, cases, photographs, diagrams, and fonts have documented rights.

## Manuscript integrity

- [ ] Substantive edit complete.
- [ ] Copy edit and final proof complete.
- [ ] All claims and citations verified.
- [ ] Permissions and anonymization checked.
- [ ] Front matter, acknowledgements, copyright, and disclaimer complete.
- [ ] No draft notes, placeholders, comments, or private identifiers remain.

## Kindle EPUB

- [ ] EPUB has correct metadata and cover.
- [ ] Navigation and table of contents work.
- [ ] Heading hierarchy is valid.
- [ ] Images have useful alternative text where appropriate.
- [ ] Links and notes work on representative devices/previews.
- [ ] Typography reflows cleanly at different font sizes.
- [ ] EPUB validation reports no blocking errors.

## Paperback interior

- [ ] Trim size, bleed, margins, and gutter are approved.
- [ ] Chapter openings, running heads, folios, and blank pages are consistent.
- [ ] Widows, orphans, bad breaks, and stranded headings are corrected.
- [ ] Images meet resolution and grayscale-legibility requirements.
- [ ] Print PDF embeds required fonts and uses the correct page size.

## Cover

- [ ] Final page count and paper choice are known before spine calculation.
- [ ] Full cover uses the current KDP template dimensions.
- [ ] Title, subtitle, author, spine, back copy, and barcode area are correct.
- [ ] Cover is inspected at thumbnail size and in print preview.

## Upload and proof

- [ ] Kindle preview reviewed on multiple form factors.
- [ ] Paperback preview reviewed page by page.
- [ ] Physical proof ordered when schedule permits.
- [ ] Upload warnings resolved or explicitly accepted.
- [ ] Final uploaded files archived with date and version.

## Launch

- [ ] Book page copy matches the final manuscript promise.
- [ ] Website, LinkedIn, lead-magnet, and outreach assets are ready.
- [ ] Claims used in launch materials are traceable to the evidence base.
- [ ] Post-publication correction and review process is assigned.
