## Review questions

- What business and relationship problem existed?
- What value did participants receive?
- Which roles and repeated interactions made the mechanism work?
- What outcome is observable or measurable?
- Which elements transfer to another organization, and which do not?
- What risks, exclusions, or failed assumptions matter?
