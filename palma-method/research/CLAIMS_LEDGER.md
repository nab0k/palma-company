# Claims Ledger

Use this ledger for consequential, quantitative, current, or easily contested claims.

| Claim ID | Exact proposed claim | Chapter | Evidence card(s) | Category | Verification | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Allowed categories:

- sourced fact;
- author interpretation;
- hypothesis;
- live observation;
- field note.

A manuscript sentence must not blur categories. Quantitative claims require original context, date, population, method, and limitations.
