# Book readiness audit v0.1

## Executive finding

The book now exists as a complete, readable working manuscript rather than a collection of chapter skeletons and research packets. The assembled build contains 23 reading units in order and approximately 38,000 words including the working bibliography.

The architecture is closed. The six canonical phases remain Orient, Diagnose, Decide, Design, Enable, and Evaluate. Part IV deliberately packages them as five reader-facing chapters — Diagnose, Design, Launch, Advise, and Handover — under the mapping fixed in the Palma Method Canon.

The manuscript is structurally ready for two bounded passes:

1. an optional Palma × Collective field note;
2. the approved author-voice line edit.

Collective is not needed to make the argument complete. It can make the book more distinctive if it supplies real consented detail, a visible decision, and a prospective test without pretending that results already exist.
