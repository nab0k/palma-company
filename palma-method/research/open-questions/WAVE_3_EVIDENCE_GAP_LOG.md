1. **Eight new source cards (SC-029 through SC-036) were added this wave, none independently full-text-verified.** Every new card's `verification_status` reflects "checked via secondary summaries/abstracts," not a primary-text read - consistent with this project's disclosure standard, but flagged collectively here as a priority for a future verification pass before manuscript finalization, in the same spirit as the original 17-card verification effort documented in the claims ledger.
2. **Seven new VI items (VI-005 through VI-011) were identified this wave** and are appended to `research/verification/claims-ledger.md` alongside the four existing items, preserving the master ledger as a single source of truth (unlike the earlier, flagged VI-002/VI-004 inconsistency, this wave's new items were added directly to the ledger rather than left only in individual chapter packets).
3. **All six chapters contain at least one editorial placeholder** in place of a real, named, verifiable case, consistent with this task's exclusion of invented field observations. None of the six is manuscript-ready until its placeholder is replaced with an approved example.
4. **Two practitioner frameworks (Cynefin, Rogers's diffusion model) and one industry-consensus standard (Barcelona Principles) are used across this wave** and are explicitly and repeatedly labeled as bounded practitioner tools or professional consensus, not validated scientific law, per this task's specific instructions.
5. **No chapter in this wave proposes a sixth Relationship Capital dimension, redefines Access, or reopens the separate Palma Method architecture workstream** - all six chapters use the approved five-dimension architecture and the CHAPTER_03_v0.2/CHAPTER_06_v0.4-synced Access definition verbatim.
