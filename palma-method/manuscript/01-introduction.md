# Introduction: The Missing Response

## Chapter question

What happens after the message lands, and why does response mark the beginning of relationship value?

## Working claim

Communication is usually measured at delivery, while strategic value begins when another person responds, returns, recommends, contributes, accepts a role, shares information, or creates something with others.

## Required elements

- A strong opening scene.
- The message-versus-response distinction.
- The open palm as a restrained symbol of visible intent and reciprocity.
- A clear promise to the reader.
- Limits: not every message needs to create a community.
