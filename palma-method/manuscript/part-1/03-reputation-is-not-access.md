# Chapter 3: Reputation Is Not Access

## Chapter question

Why does recognition not guarantee information, better terms, partnership, or opportunity?

## Working claim

Reputation can make an organization visible and credible, but access travels through relationships and network position.

## Evidence needed

- [ ] Precise distinction between reputation, trust, ties, and access.
- [ ] Evidence on information and opportunity flow.
- [ ] Examples where high visibility coexists with weak access.
