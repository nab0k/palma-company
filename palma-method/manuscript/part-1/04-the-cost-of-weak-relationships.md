# Chapter 4: The Cost of Weak Relationships

## Chapter question

What does an organization lose when relationships are fragmented, passive, or concentrated in a few people?

## Working claim

Weak relationship infrastructure creates delayed information, concentration risk, fragile continuity, poor referrals, and missed coordination.

- [ ] Causal claims distinguished from plausible managerial consequences.
