# Chapter 1: Companies Speak. People Scroll.

## Chapter question

Why does more communication fail to create stronger relationships?

## Working claim

Attention, delivery, and reach are communication outputs; they are not evidence of reciprocal relationship value.

## Evidence needed

- [ ] Current evidence on information overload, platform fatigue, and trust.
- [ ] A mechanism showing where one-way communication stops.
- [ ] A credible organizational example and a counterexample.
