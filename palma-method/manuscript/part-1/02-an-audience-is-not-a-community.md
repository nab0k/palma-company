# Chapter 2: An Audience Is Not a Community

## Chapter question

How do reach, followers, customers, stakeholders, and participants differ as social realities?

## Working claim

An audience receives attention; a community contains relationships, roles, repeated interaction, and some capacity for mutual contribution.

## Evidence needed

- [ ] Definitions that remain useful without making community sacred.
- [ ] Boundary cases and situations where an audience is sufficient.
