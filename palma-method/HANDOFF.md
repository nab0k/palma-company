## Mission

Produce a credible, useful modern management book for CEOs, founders, owners, managing partners, and senior communications leaders. The book must stand on its own while becoming the intellectual foundation of Palma's method, services, and go-to-market strategy.

## Editorial invariant

> The true unit of communication is not a message. It is a response.

## Reader outcome

The reader should be able to see relationships as organizational infrastructure and understand why active communication does not automatically create access or opportunity.

## Method under test

1. Listen.
2. Map.
3. Design.
4. Activate.
5. Measure.

The communication strategy is the palm that holds the system together. This model remains provisional until it has been tested against research and the live experiment.

## Production guardrails

- Do not overclaim results from a two-week experiment.
- Prefer primary and original sources.
- Separate facts, interpretation, hypothesis, observation, and personal experience.
- Use direct quotes only with precise page or location references.
- Use personal experience as concise field notes, not inflated case studies.
- Anonymize participant data by default and obtain permission for identifiable material.
- Do not turn the book into a Palma brochure.

When this summary and the canonical handoff differ, the canonical handoff governs unless a later entry in `DECISIONS.md` explicitly supersedes it.
